Frequent Asked Questions/Help

  1. What is Btrade?

    2. Btrade is a cryptocurrency simulation website.

  2. What is the purpose of Btrade?

    3. Btrade was created to provide novices with a platform that teaches how to trade without damaging your wallet. You learn the ins and outs of trading without using your own money.

  3. Who created Btrade?

    4. Btrade was created by a group of students called BrokerTogether, consisting of Chandler Ortman, Joseph Price, Gabriella Johnson, Felix Benavides, and Fabian De Soto.

  4. Do I have to use my own money for this website?

    5. No, the money used in this website is all made up and does not affect your financial standing.

  5. How much money will be allocated to me when I sign up?

    6. When you sign up, you will be given $10,000 to start off with.

  6. What happens when I run out of money?

    7. You will not receive anymore money, to get more money you would have to sell your assets.

  7. I need to change my profile info. Where do I go?

    8. Go here to edit profile information.

  8. What is are the graphs for?

    9. The graphs provide a visual representation of the currencies past history. The graphs are able to provide the user with some valuable information about whether they should buy or sell.

  9. Can anyone see my information?

    10. No, the only people who can see your information are the system admin and yourself.

  10. This website is confusing. Is there a tutorial or something to help?

    11. On any given page, there is a blue question mark in the top right corner. Click the button and it will explain all the aspects on that page.
